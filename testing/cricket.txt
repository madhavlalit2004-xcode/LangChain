The Spirit of Cricket

Upon the field of emerald green,
Where hopes are high and hearts are keen,
A leather ball, a willow bat,
Begin a tale beyond just that.

The bowler runs with steady grace,
Determination on each face.
The batter stands with watchful eyes,
Prepared to chase the open skies.

A cover drive, so smooth and bright,
The crowd erupts in pure delight.
A soaring six beyond the stand,
A moment etched across the land.

The fielders dive with fearless might,
Turning day into a thrilling fight.
Each catch, each run, each wicket won,
A story told beneath the sun.

Yet cricket's more than score or fame,
Or lifting high a golden name.
It's respect before the contest starts,
And sportsmanship within our hearts.

For victory fades with passing years,
As echoes soften from the cheers.
But courage, friendship, grit, and grace,
Leave lasting footprints every place.

So let the bat and ball unite,
In morning's glow or under lights.
For cricket is a timeless art—
A game that plays within the heart.
